Who are we?

E.A.R.L has been delivering hand-crafted gourmet sandwiches to Kaimuki and surrounding communities since 2014! We use fresh, high-quality ingredients, our bread is baked daily, and we source locally whenever possible. E.A.R.L is more than your ordinary sandwich shop: with our signature ingredients, house-made spreads, and unique flavor combinations, our goal is to turn lunch into the best part of the day. With quick friendly service, a product we believe in, and affordable prices, we think we’re going in the right direction! If you can’t make it into the store during our regular hours, look for us at local markets and specialty events all over the island or let us cater your next event.

Locally Owned with Aloha

Since 2016, Justin Parvizimotl, has been focused on creating long-term value, making it a privately-held company owned and operated in the up and coming Kakaako district on Oahu, Hawaii.
